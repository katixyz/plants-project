Hi, I am Kati Zech!

Plant enthusiast, based in Cologne

My own journey in urban gardening started during my studies in 2016, when I got my first green plant to take care of: a ficus benajmini. Unfortunately, it was sick from the beginning, so I had to find out how to save it and luckily it worked out and today it is one of my largest plants. So nowadays, it is a concern to me to give all the necessary tips and tricks in a clear way to you to become a succesful urban gardener.
